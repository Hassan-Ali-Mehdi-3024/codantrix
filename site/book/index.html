01FAQ · Before the call

Quick questions. Most we hear before clients book.

Q.01
What should I prepare?
A rough description of the workflow, who runs it today, and what "good" looks like at the end. If you have data volumes, system names, or a timeline pressure, bring those. If you don't, that's fine — we'll figure it out on the call.
Q.02
Can we bring our engineer?
Bring them. Two engineers in a 30-min call usually beats two non-technical stakeholders in 60. We'll go deeper on stack and architecture if there's an engineer in the room.
Q.03
What if we're not ready to commit yet?
Then we won't ask you to. The 30 minutes are to figure out fit. If it's a "later," we'll say so and follow up when your timeline shifts.
Q.04
What if we need a longer call?
Book the 30. If we need more, we'll schedule it on the spot. Most scope calls finish on time.
Q.05
How is pricing structured?
Fixed-price per engagement. 50% upfront non-refundable retainer, 50% on delivery. No hourly billing. SOW signed before any work begins. Change requests get their own SOW.
Q.06
Do you do NDAs?
Yes — mutual NDA before the call if you need it. Send yours or use ours. We sign before anything sensitive gets discussed.

Calendar full or wrong window? Email works.

If your window doesn't show up in the picker, or you'd rather start in writing, send a paragraph about the workflow you want to automate. We reply within one business day.
